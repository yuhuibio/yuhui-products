## DIY Essential Oil Insect Repellent: The Ultimate Recipe
To craft your own effective repellent, use a 4 oz clean glass spray bottle and follow this professional-grade formula:

### Ingredients List
1. **Base Liquid:** 2 ounces of distilled water.
2. **Solubilizer/Emulsifier:** 1 ounce of witch hazel or high-proof vodka (crucial for blending oils).
3. **Pure Essential Oils:** 50 to 75 drops of your preferred botanical oils (keep total oil concentration under 15% for safety).
4. **Skin Nourishment (Optional):** 0.5 ounces of Jojoba oil for moisturizing properties.

### Step-by-Step Instructions
1. **Combine:** Pour the witch hazel or vodka into your spray bottle.
2. **Mix:** Add your chosen **essential oils** and shake gently to combine.
3. **Dilute:** Top off the bottle with distilled water, leaving a small gap at the top.
4. **Shake & Apply:** Because water and oil separate naturally, **shake your DIY repellent spray well** before every single use.
