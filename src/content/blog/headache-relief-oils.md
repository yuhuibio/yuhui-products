## Essential Safety Precautions
Essential oils are highly concentrated. Before using them, please keep these safety guidelines in mind:
*   **Dilution**: Always dilute with a carrier oil (like jojoba or sweet almond oil) before topical application.
*   **Patch Test**: Apply a small amount to your inner arm to check for sensitivity.
*   **Consultation**: If you are pregnant or have existing health conditions, always consult with a professional healthcare provider.
