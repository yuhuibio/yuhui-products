### The "Purity Challenge": What Professional Buyers Must Ask
Not all essential oils are distilled equally. The extraction process for *Cyperus rotundus* (derived specifically from the **roots**) is delicate. When vetting a **Cyperus rotundus essential oil supplier** for high-end formulations, transparency is your best quality assurance.

Before finalizing your procurement, ensure your supplier provides:

1.  **GC/MS Testing:** Third-party Gas Chromatography/Mass Spectrometry (GC/MS) reports are non-negotiable. This data confirms the oil’s chemical composition, ensuring it hasn't been adulterated or diluted.
2.  **Botanical Integrity:** Verify the plant part used. The therapeutic properties are concentrated in the roots; ensure your batch is distilled from pure root stock, not stalks or leaves.
3.  **Extraction Methodology:** Steam distillation remains the gold standard for maintaining the volatile compounds that provide the oil’s signature aroma and functional benefits.
