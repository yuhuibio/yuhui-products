# 37°C Screw Cold-Pressed Sweet Almond Oil: A Premium Botanical Ingredient for Modern Cosmetics

When formulating high-performance personal care and cosmetic products, selecting the right carrier and active plant oils is critical. Among various extraction methods, low-temperature processing preserves the natural integrity of botanical compounds. This article explores the technical highlights and formulation benefits of our **37°C Screw Cold-Pressed Sweet Almond Oil**.

## What is 37°C Cold-Pressed Sweet Almond Oil?

Sweet almond oil has long been a staple in skincare, aromatherapy, and cosmetic manufacturing due to its rich emollient properties, Vitamin E content, and skin-friendly fatty acids. However, the extraction technique dictates the final quality, stability, and therapeutic efficacy of the oil.

Our product utilizes a specialized **37°C screw cold-pressing process**. By strictly controlling the temperature threshold during mechanical expression, we prevent thermal degradation of the delicate nutrients naturally present in the almonds. For formulators looking to expand their portfolio, pairing this with other botanical active ingredients—such as our high-purity [Black Tea Extract Powder](/products/black-tea-extract-powder)—can elevate anti-aging and antioxidant cosmetic lines.

For B2B buyers and formulators, physical and chemical verification is the first step in quality control.

* **Authentic Color Profile:** True, pure cold-pressed sweet almond oil processed at low temperatures displays a signature natural golden-yellow hue. Blended, diluted, or heavily refined oils often lose this authentic depth and character.
* **No Additives:** Our product contains **no artificial additives, synthetic diluents, or unwanted blending agents**, ensuring 100% natural plant integrity.

## Why Choose Low-Temperature Extraction for Cosmetics?

1. **Nutrient Preservation:** High-heat extraction can destroy heat-sensitive vitamins and antioxidants. Keeping the process near 37°C protects the natural lipid profile.
2. **Enhanced Skin Affinity:** Oils extracted gently tend to feel lighter, absorb more smoothly, and offer superior moisturizing benefits without clogging pores.
3. **Clean Label Formulations:** Modern consumers demand transparency. Using pure, mechanically pressed oils allows brands to market truly clean, plant-based cosmetics, similar to the pure standards maintained in our [camellia seed oil specifications](/blog/camellia-seed-oil-specifications).

## Applications in B2B Manufacturing

* **Skincare & Facial Serums:** Acts as a lightweight carrier oil for active botanical extracts.
* **Body Lotions & Massage Oils:** Provides excellent glide and long-lasting skin hydration.
* **Hair Care Products:** Nourishes dry scalps and adds natural shine to hair formulations.
